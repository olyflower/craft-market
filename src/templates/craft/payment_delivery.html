Payment and Delivery

Information about payment methods and delivery options

Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods for your purchases:

  • Credit Card
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfer

Delivery Options

We offer the following delivery options for your orders:

  • Standard Shipping
  • Express Shipping
  • Free Shipping (on orders over $50)

Delivery Time

The estimated delivery time for your order will depend on your location and the selected delivery option. Standard shipping usually takes 5-7 business days, while express shipping can arrive within 1-3 business days.

Contact us at Contacts for more information or if you have any questions about payment and delivery.
